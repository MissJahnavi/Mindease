About MindEase

Empowering Minds, Easing Lives

Who We Are?

MindEase is a mental health support platform dedicated to providing AI-powered analysis, self-assessment tools, and expert resources to help individuals manage their well-being.We are a team of passionate second-year engineering students specializing in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. With a shared commitment to innovation, we created MindEase to harness the power of AI in addressing mental wellness.

Our Mission & Vision

We aim to break the stigma surrounding mental health by making support accessible, secure, and technology-driven.

We aim to make a community where mental health is prioritized ,destigmatized and acessible to all.

At its core Mindease aims to give hope to every person who is experencing stress , anxiety ,depression or any mental health related issue .
